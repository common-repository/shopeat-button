This plugin allows you to implement ShopEat.com API button in any of your recipes.

== Description ==

ShopEat for wordpress Plugin allows you to add ShopEat button to all your recipes in your blog.

With ShopEat wordpress plugin you add an amazing tool for your audience that helps them find the right and accurate ingredients for your recipes. Your audience enjoy the experience in your site and return to visit their favorite recipes by saving them with ShopEat.

After installing ShopEat Button your visitors can Save and Sync the ingredients of any recipe to their Smartphone with our Free iPhone APP avaliable for download in AppStore. Also you may reuse, create and manage your shopping list online from ShopEat.com website.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `shopeat` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Markup your posts using visual editor/tags
